A photo on your phone gets buried. A ticket stub sits in a drawer. SmartStubs brings them together — giving every great night a permanent, tangible place to live.

The Problem

Great Nights
Get Forgotten.

You spend months looking forward to a concert. You're front row. The energy is unreal. You take 200 photos. And then — those photos sit buried in your camera roll, never seen again.

The ticket stub ends up in your pocket, then a drawer, then gone. There's no object that holds the memory together. No easy way to share it. No moment of rediscovery.

📱
Photos on your phone
Buried under thousands of other images. Hard to find, easy to forget.
🗂️
The ticket stub
A great physical reminder that carries zero context. No photos, no story.
🤷
No connection
The physical and digital live in completely separate worlds.
The Solution

A Stub That Remembers.

SmartStubs are custom printed ticket stubs with an NFC chip embedded inside. Each one is a physical object that holds a living, permanent link to your gallery of memories.

01
📬
Receive

Order your SmartStub and receive a beautifully custom printed ticket with a blank NFC chip embedded inside.

02
🎟️
Setup

Create your account, enter your event details — artist, venue, date — and upload your favourite photos from the night.

03
📲
Write

Tap your phone to the NFC zone on your stub to write your unique gallery link directly onto the chip. Takes seconds.

04
✨
Relive

Any time you — or anyone else — taps the stub, it opens your gallery instantly. The memory never expires.

What Makes It Special

More Than A Ticket.

Physical + Digital

SmartStubs live in both worlds. Hold it in your hand, tap it with your phone. The physical and digital finally connected in one object.

Permanent Gallery

Your gallery lives at a permanent URL that never changes. The stub you program today will still work years from now.

Instantly Shareable

Hand your stub to a friend, they tap it, they see everything. No app needed, no login required to view a gallery.

Custom Printed

Every stub is printed to order with your event details. It looks and feels like a real premium ticket, not a piece of paper.

Up to 25 Photos

Upload your best shots from the night. Add a personal note. Build a gallery that actually tells the story of being there.

Works Everywhere

Concerts, sports games, festivals, theatre, travel. If it was worth being there, it's worth remembering with a SmartStub.
